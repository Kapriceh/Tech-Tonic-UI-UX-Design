Our Community Impact

Brewers Community Foundation harnesses the pride, passion and commiment of Brewers fans, players and other supporters to positvely impact the lives of children and their families in Greater Milwaukee and Wisconsin.

Lifesaving Needs

Late one winter night, Rebecca arrived at the doors of the Sojourner Family Peace Center with nothing but a small bag and quiet fear. Advocates helped her create a safety plan, connected her to counselling, and placed her and her son in a secure home where healing could begin. With job support and legal guidance, she rebuilt her independence. Stories like Rebecca’s are possible because of community investment—through grants from the Brewers Community Foundation, nonprofits can provide lifesaving shelter, advocacy, and hope when it’s needed most.

One Grant, One Trip.

In One of Milwaukee's most underprivileged neighborhoods, the Brewers Community Foundation awarded a grant to the Boys & Girls Clubs of Greater Milwaukee to expand educational opportunities for local youth. With those funds, the Club organized an HBCU college tour for 20 boys and girls, many of whom had never stepped foot on a college campus. One stop changed everything: Howard University. Inspired by the legacy, excellence, and possibilities they witnessed, two students later earned Buddy Scholarships to attend. Years later, both returned home as physicians, providing free annual checkups at the same Boys & Girls Club that once nurtured their dreams. Proof that one grant can spark lasting change in an entire community
